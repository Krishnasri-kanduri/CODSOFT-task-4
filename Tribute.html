Tribute to Dr. Srinivasan Kanduri

Dr. Srinivasan Kanduri

To the man I admire most, and for whom I always feel blessed, my father, Dr. Srinivasan Kanduri.My inspiration, my hero, and my supportsystem, he was a guiding light in my life. His wisdom, kindness, and unwavering support shaped who I am today.

A Kind and Sweethearted Soul, Renowned Astrologer and Vaastu Expert. He was excellent in Astrology and Vaastu, and his insightful Panchanga Sravanam was highly regarded. He received Sanmanam on April 13th, 2021 for his exceptional Panchanga Sravanam.

Honorary Doctorate in Astrology

Awarded on April 2nd, 2023, at the Vedic Astrology Conclave Sri Shobhakruth Nama Samvathsara Ugadi Puraskaaralu, in recognition of his extraordinary contribution to society through research and development in the field of astrology and Vaastu.
News of this honor was featured in the Indian Express.

Jyothishya Ratna Award

Received on April 30th, 2022, from Vishwajyothi Jyothishya Vignana Samsta.

Mahatma Gandhi Sanman Award

Received on March 8th, 2015, from the International Integrity Peace and Friendship Society in Karnataka. Presented by Hon'ble Revenue Minister V. Srinivas Prasad, in recognition of his outstanding services in astrology and Vaastu.

Panchanga Sravanam Sanmanam - 13-04-2021
